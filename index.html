About the Author

Author Tommy Nocerino, a native New Yorker with expertise in American history and American cinema, is often called on as a guest speaker on various subjects. His pop culture commentary is also in demand on numerous internet radio shows. Tommy resides in Fernandina Beach, Florida, and is a teacher of various subjects. With three well-received novels published, he continues to foster and refine his many interests and continues writing.

About Book

Tommy Nocerino’s new book “The Mysterious Mr. M.” follows Principal Rudolph Blankenship as he deals with a series of weird occurrences at Atlantic View High School in Florida. From a fire in the science building to stuff with a substitute teacher named Mr. M. Blankenship has to navigate the unexpected and the supernatural. As the school community faces disasters and things get wild Mr. M. brings calm and positivity and wins over students and staff. The book is about leadership, resilience and the power of kindness in the face of chaos. The story is a series of unexplained events: a collapsed cafeteria ceiling, a fire in the field house and a swarm of wasps at football practice. As things get nuttier and more suspicious Mr. M. is the calm in the storm offering advice and support to those who need it. With a mix of supernatural and heartwarming moments “The Mysterious Mr. M.” is about the power of kindness and what one person can do for a whole community. You’ll be wondering about the mysterious Mr. M. and what’s going on at Atlantic View High School.

What keeps you Turn the pages?

The author’s skillful storytelling and shiny descriptions deliver the putting of Atlantic View High School to existence, immersing readers in its halls and classrooms. The interactions between the characters, especially the dynamic among Principal Blankenship and Mr. M, add intensity and complexity to the story. As the thriller unfolds, readers are stored on the edge in their seats, seeking to piece together the clues and get to the bottom of the truth in the back of Mr. M’s identification and his connection to the faculty. The novel’s sudden twists and turns hold readers guessing till the very quit, making “The Mysterious Mr. M.” an exciting and fulfilling study.
